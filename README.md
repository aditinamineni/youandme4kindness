# You & Me 4 Kindness - Nonprofit E-Commerce Website

Welcome to the official website for **You & Me 4 Kindness**, a student-led social enterprise creating handmade scrunchies with a purpose.

## 🎭 Our Mission

To celebrate cultural heritage while creating opportunities for individuals of all abilities to thrive through creative expression. Every scrunchie is handcrafted from authentic Indian fabrics, and **100% of net profits are donated** to support inclusive theater programs through North Texas Performing Arts (NTPA).
