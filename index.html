Empowering Every Star Through Creativity, Culture, and Community

Through handmade scrunchies, inclusive dance programs, and accessible resources, we help individuals with special needs discover confidence, joy, self-expression, and belonging.

You & Me 4 Kindness was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves opportunities to shine. By combining cultural heritage, artistic expression, and community support, we create meaningful experiences that empower individuals of all abilities.

Three Ways We Make a Difference

Every initiative works together to support individuals with special needs while building a more inclusive community.

Handmade Scrunchies

Every scrunchie is carefully handmade using vibrant fabrics sourced from India. Each purchase helps fund inclusive programs while celebrating culture, creativity, and kindness.
